How It Works

Four simple steps to secure your crypto inheritance on the Solana blockchain

01

Create Your Will

Set a timeout period (e.g. 90 days) and grace period. Your dead man's switch is now active on-chain.

02

Deposit & Assign

Deposit SOL into your vault and designate beneficiaries with percentage shares. Up to 10 beneficiaries supported.

03

Check In Regularly

Periodically check in to reset the timer. Each check-in proves you're still active and resets the countdown.

04

Automatic Inheritance

If you stop checking in, after timeout + grace period, beneficiaries can claim their designated share of the vault.

Powerful Features

Everything you need to manage your crypto inheritance with confidence

🔒

Dead Man's Switch

Configurable timeout with grace period. Your will activates only after prolonged inactivity.

👥

Up to 10 Beneficiaries

Add multiple beneficiaries with precise share allocation in basis points (0.01% precision).

⏸️

Pause & Resume

Going on vacation? Pause your will to freeze the timer. Unpause when you're back.

💸

Flexible Withdrawals

Withdraw your deposited SOL anytime before timeout. Full control while you're active.

🔄

Transfer Ownership

Transfer your will to a new wallet address if you need to change your primary wallet.

📡

On-Chain Events

Every action emits on-chain events for full transparency and audit trail.
